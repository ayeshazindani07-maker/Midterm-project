Creamy Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 100g Cheese
  • 2 cloves Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil pasta for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat milk and garlic in a pan.
  3. Add cheese and stir well.
  4. Mix with pasta and serve hot.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Pour into a baking pan.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes.

Fresh Salad

" width="300">

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed lettuce leaves
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the lettuce leaves.
  2. Combine lettuce, cucumber, tomato, and onion in a bowl.
  3. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  4. Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently.
  5. Top with crumbled feta cheese and serve fresh.

Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onion and garlic.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in vegetable broth and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Blend until smooth and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve hot with bread or croutons.

Cheesy Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms
  • 1/2 tsp oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Spread pizza sauce on the base.
  3. Add bell peppers, mushrooms, and mozzarella cheese.
  4. Sprinkle oregano on top.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until cheese melts.

Juicy Burger

Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 200g beef or chicken patty
  • 2 cheese slices
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp ketchup

Steps

  1. Grill or pan-fry the burger patties until cooked.
  2. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  3. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on the buns.
  4. Layer lettuce, tomato, onion, patty, and cheese.
  5. Cover with the top bun and serve warm.
